About

Privacy Policy

CHOMEI COMMERZ LTD ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you interact with our website and services.

Information we collect: When you contact us via email or our communication platforms, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your messages. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiries and provide our services.

Use of data: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except as required to deliver our services or comply with legal obligations.

WhatsApp Business: When you communicate with us via WhatsApp, messages are processed through the WhatsApp Business API. Your data is handled in accordance with WhatsApp's terms of service and our obligations under UK data protection law.

Data retention: We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Your rights: Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at info@chomei.store for any data-related requests.
