About

The lab arm of Chomei Commerz

BOMALAB is the digital innovation lab of Chomei Commerz Ltd. We focus on applied AI, community-facing platforms, and the technical infrastructure that powers them.

We work at the intersection of technology and social impact — building tools that are accessible, culturally sensitive, and built to last. From AI assistants that speak multiple languages to platforms that connect communities across borders.

Every project starts with the question: what does this community actually need?
